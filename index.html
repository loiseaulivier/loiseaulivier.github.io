Research

My research investigates the formal properties of natural language, drawing on fieldwork, experimental methods, and cross-linguistic comparison. Current projects include:

Syntax & Phrase Structure

How clauses are built from smaller pieces, and what this reveals about universal grammar.

Morphosyntax

The interaction between word-internal structure and syntactic derivations.

Semantics & Pragmatics

Compositionality, scope, tense, and how meaning is assembled from form.

Language X Fieldwork

Documenting and analysing [Language name], an under-described language of [region].
